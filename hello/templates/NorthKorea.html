North Korea, country in East Asia. It occupies the northern portion of the Korean peninsula,
which juts out from the Asian mainland between the East Sea
(Sea of Japan) and the Yellow Sea; North Korea covers about 55
percent of the peninsula’s land area. The country is bordered by
China and Russia to the north and by the Republic of Korea (South Korea)
to the south. The national capital, P’yŏngyang, is a major industrial and
transport centre near the west coast.
