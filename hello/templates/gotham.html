Gotham is a TV series based on James Gordon's early days as a policeman
in Gotham City and Bruce Wayne's path to becoming Batman, as well as the
origins of several of Batman's rogues' gallery such as Penguin, Riddler,
Catwoman, Scarecrow, Mad Hatter, Bane, Ventriloquist and the Joker.
The series ran for five seasons which aired from September 2014 to April 2019.
