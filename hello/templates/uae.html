The United Arab Emirates (UAE; in Arabic: الإمارات العربية المتحدة)
is a country in Western Asia. It borders Oman, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia.
The capital is Abu Dhabi, and the largest city is Dubai. The Burj Khalifa,
Earth's tallest artificial structure,[8] is in Dubai.
Approximately 600.83 square kilometers is the area of the United Arab Emirates,
till December 31, 2017, the UAE counted a population of 9,304,277,
according to the reports of the Federal Competitiveness and
Statistics Authority.
